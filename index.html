How the Operating Layer Works

Intake

Every inquiry is captured and responded to — at 1AM or 1PM. No lead waits. No DM goes cold. The intake process qualifies clients before they reach the calendar.

Deposit

The deposit request goes out automatically. The payment link is clean and professional. The client does not wait. The deposit proves intent. Intent protects your time.
